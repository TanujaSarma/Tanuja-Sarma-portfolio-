Historical Horizons

Historical Horizons is a website developed by me and my teammate(s) to showcase archaeological sites, historical landmarks, and cultural heritage. The site features an immersive image gallery, articles, and a forum to explore and discuss ancient civilizations and architectural wonders. Working on this project gave us hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We went through phases like designing wireframes, implementing responsive layouts, styling galleries, building interactive elements (like the gallery and forum), debugging cross-browser issues, and collaborating as a team. It was a valuable learning journey, sharpening both our technical skills and our teamwork.
